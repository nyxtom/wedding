Out of Town Guests

Since most of you will be traveling from out of state, there are a few things to consider when planning your trip before, during and after. This includes local transportation, airport transportation, hotel/lodging and of course your flights.

Airport Transportation:  If you are in need of transportation to or from the airport (Salt Lake City Airport), we recommend making your own arrangements. A few of the available services are listed below. (if the rates are too expensive, please contact us ahead of time so arrangements can be made).

  • All Resort Group or contact at 877-658-3999
  • Aspen Transportation or contact at 888-465-7736
  • Canyon Transportation: contact at 800-255-1841

Local Transportation:  If you are coming from the Holiday Inn, you will need to arrange local transportation from either Aspen Transportation, Ace Cab Company or other local taxi companies available in the area. Homestead Resort (and Zermatt Resort) offers shuttle services to its guests for local transportation within Midway, Heber City and Park City. You can contact the Homestead Resort (or Zermatt Resort) concierge service desk for availability and scheduling. Since there are a number of guests attending the ceremony, it would be best to make arrangements ahead of time. Alternatively, if you have rented a car for your visit, Johnson Mill Inn will provide parking services when you arrive.

Local Airport: Salt Lake City International Airport

Depending on where you are booking from, you may wish to book some time in advance due to the fluxuating rates. If you are flying in from Miami, you may find good rates through Google Flights - Miami to SLC. The airfare will vary depending on the dates and times you arrive and depart. Google Flights provides a graph to help visualize the best times to come in and depart. For some guests that are apart of the bridal party, they will need to fly as soon as Thursday evening or Friday morning. Guests from around the country may find good results by using this tool when booking their travels - Google Flights
